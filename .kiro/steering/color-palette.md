# MarketMojo.ai Color Palette

## Primary Colors (Trust and Energy)
- **Deep Teal** — `#01546C` — from "Market" in logo
- **Vibrant Aqua** — `#00A6BF` — from "Mojo" in logo
- **Accent Teal** — `#018C99` — mix/bridge color between Deep Teal and Vibrant Aqua

## Secondary / Neutral Colors (Structure and Clarity)
- **Cool Slate Grey** — `#6B7D8C` — for text and subtle details, inspired by ".ai"
- **Soft Whisper Grey** — `#F9FAFB` — base background
- **Mid-tone Grey** — `#A1B0B9` — for borders and disabled states

## Action / Status Colors
- **Success Green** — `#2ECC71` — for positive metrics and actions
- **Warning Amber** — `#F1C40F` — for alerts
- **Market Surge Red** — `#E05D5D` — for key action buttons and engagement

## Usage Guidelines
- **Primary:** Deep Teal & Vibrant Aqua for branding, headers, primary buttons
- **Secondary/Neutral:** Cool Slate & Whisper Greys for structure and clarity
- **Action/Status:** Success Green, Warning Amber, Market Surge Red for engagement and feedback

## Score Gauge Colors
- Score >= 60: Success Green (`#2ECC71`)
- Score 30-59: Warning Amber (`#F1C40F`)
- Score < 30: Market Surge Red (`#E05D5D`)
